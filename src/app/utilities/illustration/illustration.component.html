Illustration

Our illustration style builds off the simple shapes of our logo. Simple shapes, clean lines and playful colors give our illustration a impactful feel and make it easy to digest and understand at a glance.

Principles

1.

Enterprise software can be playful and modern

2.

The future is here

3.

Abstract and metaphorical

Types

Spot illustration

Used in smaller moments, usually paired with copy as visual aid so that the content can be easily understood.

Hero illustration

Used in high impact moments, and can be used as background with overlaid copy.

Permission illustrations

Used to bring traditionally frustrating moments a little sense of humor: 404 pages, forbidden, restricted access, etc.
