No gutters

The gutters between columns in our predefined grid classes can be removed with .no-gutters-[size]. This removes the negative margins from .row and the horizontal padding from all immediate children columns.

  • .no-gutters - removing the gutters for all devices.
  • .no-gutters-xs - removing the gutters for xs devices only.
  • .no-gutters-sm - removing the gutters for sm devices and down.
  • .no-gutters-md - removing the gutters for md devices and down.
  • .no-gutters-lg - removing the gutters for lg devices and down.
  • .no-gutters-xl - removing the gutters for xl devices and down.
